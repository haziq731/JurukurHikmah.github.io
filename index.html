About Jurukur Hikmah

Founded in 2005, Jurukur Hikmah has established itself as a leading land surveying company committed to delivering precise, reliable, and innovative surveying solutions. With over 20 years of industry experience, our team of certified professionals combines technical expertise with cutting-edge technology to provide exceptional service.

We take pride in our attention to detail, commitment to accuracy, and dedication to client satisfaction. Our comprehensive approach ensures that each project, regardless of size or complexity, receives the highest level of care and precision.
